
# Anime
The following information pertains to anime-themed stories. This information is only relevant if the story is tagged with "Anime" and will be ignored otherwise.

##  Relationship Archetypes:
Relationship archetypes are common character tropes found in various forms of media, particularly in anime, manga, and visual novels. These archetypes describe different ways characters express their affection and interact with their love interests. When a character is assigned an archetype it should influence or override their alignement temperament, especially in romantic situations.

|Type|Gender|Description|
|----|------|-----------|
|Aho|Male/Female|Characterized by their airheadedness and silliness. They are often carefree, clumsy, and not very bright, but their innocence and genuine affection make them endearing. |
|Bifauxnen|Female|A female character who presents herself in a masculine way, often dressing and behaving like a male. She may have a strong, assertive personality and is often admired for her confidence and independence. |
|Bodere|Male/Female|Exhibits a rough, tough, or delinquent exterior but has a soft, caring side that they reveal to their love interest. They may act rebellious or aggressive but are protective and loyal. |
|Bokukko|Female|A tomboyish girl who is energetic, athletic, and often prefers traditionally masculine activities. She may struggle with her femininity but is confident in her abilities. |
|Bukako|Female|A female servant or maid character who is devoted to serving and caring for their master / love interest. They are often humble, polite, and attentive to their partner's needs. |
|Chugokujyo|Female|A female character who embodies traditional Chinese aesthetics and culture. She may wear traditional Chinese clothing and exhibit traits associated with Chinese femininity, such as grace and elegance. Tend to know martial arts.|
|Chunibyo|Male/Female|Characterized by delusions of grandeur, often believing they possess special powers or are part of a fantastical world. They may act dramatically and have an over-the-top personality, but their passion and creativity can be charming. |
|Cool Beauty|Female|A character who is calm, composed, and often admired for their beauty and elegance. They may appear aloof or distant but have a strong sense of self and inner strength. |
|Dameko|Male/Female|A character who spends a lot of time on vices, such as drinking or gambling, often to escape from reality. They may have a carefree attitude. They tend to be a bit of a burden on those around them, but their charm can make them endearing. |
|Dandere|Male/Female|Very shy, quiet, and withdrawn. They may be socially anxious or fearful, but can open up and become more talkative around someone they trust. |
|Darudere|Male/Female|Appears listless, dull, and lacking energy appearing apathetic until they become lovestruck and affectionate towards someone. |
|Deredere|Male/Female|Consistently sweet, cheerful, and openly affectionate towards their love interest. |
|Dojikko|Female|A clumsy and accident-prone female character who often finds herself in humorous or embarrassing situations. Despite her clumsiness, she is endearing and lovable. |
|Donkan|Male/Female|A character who is slow to understand romantic feelings or social cues. They may be oblivious to their love interest's affections but are often kind-hearted and genuine. |
|Dorodere|Male/Female|Starts with a sweet, deredere exterior but has dark or "murky" thoughts (like hatred or vindictiveness) hidden inside. |
|Eronna|Female|A character usually older than the protagonist who is sexually experienced and often teases or flirts with younger characters. They may have a playful or seductive personality. Frequently are doctors, teachers, secretaries, or mentors.|
|Hajidere|Male/Female|Extremely shy and easily embarrassed, especially when it comes to romantic situations. They may stutter, blush, or even pass out when around their love interest. |
|Hakase|Male/Female|A genius or prodigy character who is highly intelligent and knowledgeable in a specific field. They may be socially awkward or eccentric but are often endearing due to their passion for their work. Almost always involved in scientific or academic pursuits.|
|Haraguro|Male/Female|A character who appears sweet and innocent on the outside but harbors dark, twisted thoughts or intentions. They may be manipulative or cunning, using their charm to achieve their goals.|
|Harepeko|Male/Female|A character who is always hungry and obsessed with food. They may have a voracious appetite and are often seen eating or thinking about food, which can lead to humorous situations.|
|Hetare|Male/Female|Lack basic skills in nearly everything. If left to their own devices, they will likely starve or get into trouble. They need constant support and guidance from others to navigate daily life.|
|Himedere|Female|Acts like a princess, expecting to be treated like royalty, but may soften over time with their love interest. |
|Hinadere|Male/Female|A character with a twisted often negative view of the world. They may be cynical, pessimistic, or have a dark sense of humor, often using sarcasm to cope with their feelings. They are often critical of others.|
|Ijimekko|Female|A character that like likes to bully the main character. They may tease, taunt, or play pranks on their love interest. Possibly befriending them later on.|
|Imouto - A|Female|A younger sister character who is cute, innocent, and often looks up to their older sibling or love interest. They may be playful and affectionate, sometimes causing jealousy in others. Not all imouto are biological sisters.|
|Imouto - B|Female|A character often has speech quirks or other moe traits, making them endearing and cute. May see those who help them as a big brother or sister figure.|
|Imouto - C|Female|A big sister type character who is protective, caring, and nurturing towards their younger sibling or love interest. They may take on a maternal role and offer guidance and support.|
|Kamidere|Male/Female|Has a god complex, believing they are superior to others. They may be arrogant and demanding but can show a softer side to their love interest. |
|Kanedere|Male/Female|Materialistic and obsessed with wealth and luxury. They may prioritize money and status over genuine relationships.|
|Kawaiiko|Female|A character who is extremely cute and adorable, often using their charm to win over others. They may have a bubbly childish personality and a love for all things cute. Frequently seen with cute animals or accessories. Tend to dress like a little girl. Can be stubborn and get upset or emotional over trivial things.|
|Kimoiko|Female|A character who is socially awkward and often behaves in a way that others find creepy or unsettling. They may have unusual interests or mannerisms that set them apart from their peers. Despite this they often have a cuter side that endears them to others.|
|Kuudere|Male/Female|Appears cool, calm, and collected, showing little emotion. They are often reserved but will eventually reveal a softer, caring side to their love interest. |
|Mayadere|Male/Female|A character is initially opposed or trying to kill the protagonist but gradually develops feelings for them. Over time they may become less hostile, but still threaten the protagonist or those that get in their way.|
|Megadere|Male/Female|Has an intense adoration for their love interest and is not afraid to show it with grand displays of affection. |
|Nipadere|Male/Female|Fills their love interest with contagious joy, happiness, and positivity, always cheering them on with a bright smile. |
|Nisetoshi|Male/Female|A character who is decades or centuries old but appears much younger, often due to supernatural reasons. They may have wisdom and experience beyond their apparent age but tend to act like a child.|
|Nyandere|Male/Female|A character who exhibits cat-like traits, such as playfulness, curiosity, and affection. They may purr, meow, or display other feline behaviors, often adding a cute and endearing quality to their personality.|
|Onee-san - A|Female|Mature, nurturing, and caring older sister figure who provides guidance and support to younger characters. They may be protective and affectionate, often acting like a big sister.|
|Onee-san - B|Female|A free-spirited older sister figure who enjoys drinking and fast living. Defined by their cool demeanor and rough personalities.|
|Onee-san - C|Female|A nurturing and maternal older sister figure who takes care of others. They may be gentle, kind, and supportive, often acting like a motherly figure.|
|Orekko|Female|A character who is obsessed with being manly and often tries to hide their feminine traits. Often engage in fighting, body training, or other traditionally masculine activities.|
|Osananajimi|Male/Female|A childhood friend who has known the protagonist for a long time. They may have a deep bond and understanding of each other, often leading to romantic tension as they grow older.|
|Otakko|Male/Female|A character who is deeply passionate about anime, manga, video games, or other aspects of otaku culture. They may be socially awkward but are enthusiastic and knowledgeable about their interests.|
|Otokonoko|Male|A male character who dresses and behaves in a feminine manner, often blurring traditional gender roles. They may have a cute and charming personality, often surprising others with their appearance and demeanor.|
|Oujidere|Male|Acts like a prince, expecting to be treated like royalty, but may soften over time with their love interest. |
|Oujidere|Female|A wealthy, elegant, and refined young lady who often comes from a noble or aristocratic background. They may have a sophisticated demeanor and expect high standards from those around them. Unlike Himedere, they are not spoiled or demanding. Despite coming from wealth, they are kind and sweet.|
|Sadodere|Male/Female|Enjoys teasing, dominating, or inflicting pain on their love interest often but not always in a playful manner. They may have a mischievous personality and take pleasure in seeing their partner flustered or submissive.|
|Shojo|Female|A pure, innocent, and idealistic young girl who often embodies traditional feminine virtues. They may be kind-hearted, gentle, and optimistic, sometimes to a fault.|
|Tsundere - A|Male/Female|Acts cold, hostile, or distant to hide their true feelings of love. Over time, they may become more honest about their affection. |
|Tsundere - B|Male/Female|Acts cold, hostile, or distant to hide their true feelings of love. Unlike Tsundere - A, they come around more quickly but also quickly revert to their aloof behavior.|
|Tsunpure|Male/Female|A character who is dishonest with themselves about their feelings, often pretending to be indifferent or unaffected. They may act harshly towards their love interest only to apologize moments later.|
|Tsunshin|Male/Female|A character who is initially hostile or indifferent towards the protagonist but gradually warms up to them over time. They may be reluctant to show their feelings but eventually become more affectionate. Often regretting their initial behavior.|
|Tsuntsun|Male/Female|Unlike a Tsundere, they tend to never truly acknowledge their feelings, often acting indifferent or dismissive towards their love interest.|
|Undere|Male/Female|Submissive and obedient, often putting their love interest's needs above their own. They may struggle with self-worth but find comfort in their relationship. |
|Utsudere|Male/Female|A character who is often melancholic, gloomy, or depressed. They may struggle with self-esteem or emotional issues but can find solace and happiness in their relationship. Often bullied or struggling at home. They tend to be wary of others and may have difficulty trusting people.|
|Yamato Nadeshiko|Female|Embodies the idealized traditional Japanese woman, characterized by grace, elegance, and a strong sense of duty. They may be skilled in various traditional arts and often prioritize family and harmony. They tend to speak very formally and politely, embodying the ideals of femininity in Japanese culture.|
|Yandere|Male/Female|Becomes obsessive and dangerous due to their intense love for someone. They may appear outwardly normal but become volatile and violent if their love interest is threatened or taken by another. |
|Yangire|Male/Female|Similar to yandere, but their violent tendencies stem from trauma or mental illness rather than love. They may lash out unpredictably, even towards those they care about.|
